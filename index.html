Shrimp fried rice is a popular dish in Chinese cuisine that consists of cooked rice stir-fried with shrimp, eggs, vegetables, and seasonings. It is a quick and easy way to use up leftover rice and transform it into a delicious meal.

Pork fried rice is another popular dish in Chinese cuisine that consists of cooked rice stir-fried with pork, eggs, vegetables, and seasonings. It is a great way to use up leftover pork or rice and create a satisfying meal.

Yakimeshi is a Japanese term for fried rice, which is a dish of cooked rice stir-fried with eggs, meat, vegetables and seasonings. It is similar to the Chinese fried rice, but it uses Japanese short-grain rice, which has a sticky and chewy texture.

>20 mins

Kimchi fried rice is a spicy and savory Korean dish that combines cooked rice with fermented cabbage, meat, vegetables and seasonings. It is a quick and easy way to use up leftover ingredients and enjoy a satisfying meal.

View
10 - 30 mins

Chicken teriyaki fried rice is a Japanese-inspired dish that features stir-fried rice with chicken, vegetables and a sweet and savory teriyaki sauce. It is a simple and delicious way to enjoy a balanced meal with protein, carbs and veggies.

25 - 50 mins

Nasi goreng is a fried rice dish from Indonesia, Malaysia and Singapore. It is made by stir-frying cooked rice with various ingredients, such as eggs, meat, seafood, vegetables, spices and sauces. Nasi goreng is a dish that can be enjoyed for all meals.

20 - 45 mins
